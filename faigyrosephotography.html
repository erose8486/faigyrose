HI!

My name is Faigy Rosenberg, I’m a still-life photographer from Brooklyn.

My passion for photography started at a young age when I received my first disposable camera at age 9.
Since then I have been photographing everything in sight.
I took my photography to the next level when I joined NYIP and graduated in 2016.
When I discovered still-life photography, I was hooked.
What I love most about still-life is the way I can create a story with one image.
Whether it’s through props or lighting, each image is a new challenge and one that I fully embrace in order to ensure that you receive the best possible image of your product.
